FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your response time for inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, you can call us directly or reach out via WhatsApp.

Do you provide on-site installation services?

Yes, we provide complete on-site installation, commissioning, and testing services across India. Our experienced technicians ensure smooth implementation.

What brands do you deal with?

We are authorized dealers for OMRON, Mitsubishi Electric, Siemens, Schneider Electric, YASKAWA, Autonics, Pro-face, Leuze, and Crompton.

Do you offer AMC services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) services with preventive maintenance, emergency support, and regular system checks.

How long does project implementation take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Small projects: 1-2 weeks, Medium: 2-4 weeks, Large: 1-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide training for our staff?

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs on PLC programming, SCADA operation, system maintenance, and troubleshooting for your technical team.
